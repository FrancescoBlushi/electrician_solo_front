Current opportunities

Below are examples of the types of roles we may recruit for. Even if we do not have an active vacancy listed, you can still send us your CV and we will keep it on file.

Qualified electrician (self-employed or employed)

We look for experienced electricians who can diagnose faults, carry out repairs and complete installations to a high standard with good customer communication.

Ideal profile

  • Relevant electrical qualifications and up-to-date knowledge of regulations
  • Experience in domestic and/or commercial work
  • Clean, tidy and polite with customers
  • Full UK driving licence (preferred)

Apprentice / improver electrician

From time to time we may offer opportunities for apprentices or improvers to gain experience alongside qualified electricians.

What we look for

  • Focus on safety and compliance at all times
  • Clear and honest communication with customers and colleagues
  • Ability to work independently and manage your own jobs
  • A positive attitude and willingness to help
